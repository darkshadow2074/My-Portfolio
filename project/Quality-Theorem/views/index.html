Our History

Quality Theorem is a path breaker in the areas of Internet Of Things (IoT), Cloud Computing, Big Data, Embedded Programming and Data Analytics and, has positioned itself to be a go-to provider in the upcoming areas – M2M, Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Robotics and Industry 4.0. We develop products containing the above technologies, which are deployed in many Smart Solutions such as Smart Water, Smart Waste, Smart Effluent, Smart Health, Smart Energy and other Smart Solutions.


Our engagement with our customers are of varied nature based on the needs of the customer. We develop products required by them or we can take up an end-to-end project/ solution delivery. Also, we can provide professional/ consulting services for a specific need. We also offer technology training and mentoring services. These trainings can be online (remote) and onsite corporate training.
